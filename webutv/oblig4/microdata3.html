Anno 2205 is a city-building and economic simulation game, with real-time strategy elements. It is being developed by Ubisoft Blue Byte and published by Ubisoft. 2205 is the sixth game of the Anno series, and is planned for release on 3 November 2015. As with Anno 2070, the game is set in thefuture , with players having the opportunity to set up colonies onthe moon .
